ABOUT ME

Jordon Burton grew up in Bluefield, West Virginia as the youngest of two brothers. After gradutating High School he headed west to BYU in Provo, UT. After graduating with a degree in Spanish, he headed even further west, to Los Angeles, CA. Here he chased a long time dream to be an actor. When his father was diagnosed with cancer, he moved back to the east coast to be closer to home. In Charlotte, he met the love of his life and settled down. He currently lives with his wife and two boys. However, with an unfufilled professional life, Jordon now finds himself in a coding course, creating a Student Bio.
